Work experiences

I designed logos for different companies mainly on Fiverr with Procreate and Vectornator. Moreover I continued the architect profession by making 1:10 drawings for technical carpentery details, production of seciurity boards for small urban areas.

I worked all-around on the updating projects of REBA Palace: a 6-floors luxury hotel in Courmayeur co-designed with Kengo Kuma Architects Associated. My task was to assist the chief architect with the meetings with engineers, clients and public offices in order to ensure the project feasibility, to bring ideas and make important design choices on facades and interiors, to model the project on ArchiCad and AutoCad, create renders with Twinmotion, creating the layouts with InDesign and Photoshop, producing technical drawings at 1:100, 1:50 and 1:20 (details) scale and managing the other architects for other small tasks.

My main task was to make te feasibility study for several buildings' renovation. The study included: analysis of surveys made by external studios, cost-analysis of the project, creation of drawings 1:100 scale and layout.

I assisted the main architect on: surveys, building design, client-architect meetings, municipality-architect meetings, presentation of projects, management of architectural conventions. The programs used for the tasks were mainly: Office package, Adobe package, AutoCad.

I worked on building renovations from the survey to the design phase.

Studies

The Meta course for Front-End Developer, a 160 hours online course in which I learned the main charateristics, technologies and skills necessary to start a career as Front-End Developer. The main topics of the coure were: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, GitHub.

Master in Spatial Planning for Resiliecne. Methods and techniques on integrate risks management approach. During this master (1y) were analyzed topics related to spatial planning, such as urban planning, GBIs, mobility, sociology, decisional and economical analysis methodologies. The final dissertation was an analysis based on cost analysis of several bicycle paths typologies in relation to the economical savings on public area which will happen because of a reduction of stree-accidents thanks to the path itself.

Master's degree in Architecture for the Sustainable Design. During this period I continued my studies on architecture and urban planning by increasing the level of focus on sustainability topics applied on the AEC. The path ended with a thesis on refugee hospitality in Italy, which analyzes the 2019 situation on the topic (considering national and international legislation, scientific bibliography and realized projects).

During my Bachelor's in Architecture Science I was introduced to architecture with a multi-disciplinary methodology, which kept in consideration Architecture field in relation with mechanics, thermodynamics, economics, history, sustainability.
